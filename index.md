## Research

I am passionate about solving representation learning problems with limited supervision, which leads to two major approaches of interest: adapting to few/no supervision, and transferring knowledge. I am curious about what is happening during pre-training, how to interpret learned representations, and how to improve reliability and robustness.

My current research interests include **self-supervised learning, multi-task learning, autoencoders, domain adaptation, diffusion models**, and so on. I am seeking PhD position starting fall 2023.
